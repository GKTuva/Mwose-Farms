Who We Are

Modern East African farm.
Rooted in quality & science.

Mwose Farms is a vertically integrated goat and sheep operation based in Kenya. We combine traditional East African husbandry with modern feed science — including super Napier (Pakchong) silage, brewers' grain, and maize silage — to produce premium animals, meat, and by-products.

Goat & Sheep Rearing

We raise Dorper, Boer, and local breeds in slatted-floor, ventilated housing for optimal health and growth. Full biosecurity and vaccination protocols.

Livestock Sourcing

Trusted network across Kenya and East Africa. We source quality breeding stock and slaughter-ready animals for individuals, restaurants, and cooperatives.

Premium Cuts & Tallow

Freshly butchered mutton and chevon in premium packaging. Pure rendered sheep tallow for culinary and cosmetic use — a clean, natural alternative.

Farm Setup Consulting

End-to-end farm design: slatted housing, ventilation, water systems, feed planning, vaccination schedules, and breed selection tailored to your land and budget.
